Highlights (3–5 bullets, ≤ 85 caracteres c/u — archivo separado para Elsevier)

- Spatial ABM engine in Rust: grid, network and continuous space under one API
- Compiler-enforced synchronous update removes read-after-write by construction
- Per-agent deterministic RNG: parallel results bit-identical to sequential
- 2–5x faster than Agents.jl, 45–184x than Mesa; decide scales ~5x on 16 cores
- Reproducible across x86-64 and WebAssembly; open source
